How It Works
01
Public Data Sources
We pull from NHTSA FARS, FHWA HPMS, ARDOT CRIS, Census ACS, GTFS feeds, and other public datasets — or work directly from data you provide.
02
Automated Pipeline
Custom scripts fetch, clean, and format data into GeoJSON. Scheduled automation keeps hosted maps current when source data updates.
03
Built for Your Jurisdiction
Every map is scoped to your city, county, or MPO boundary. Data is filtered, labeled, and contextualized for your audience — not generic.
04
Hosted or Handed Off
We host it on your subdomain with automatic updates, or package it as a standalone file your team deploys on your own infrastructure.
